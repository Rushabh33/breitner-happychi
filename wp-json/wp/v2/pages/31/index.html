About Tai Chi<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

Tai Chi exercises your whole body and mind. Practiced diligently, Tai Chi can improve your circulation, flexibility and balance leading to improved health and sense of greater vitality. Sometimes called a \u201cmoving meditation\u201d it is also beneficial for your mind, as you learn and practice new things while moving.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Research from Harvard Medical School<\/a> supports that Tai Chi exercises may lead to greater muscle strength and flexibility. Additional research shows evidence connecting Tai Chi with increased balance control<\/a> and lessened chronic pain<\/a>, particularly for cases of osteoarthritis, fibromyalgia, and lower back pain.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
